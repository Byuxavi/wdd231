Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

What information do you need to start a project?

To get started, I'll need information about your business goals, target audience, design preferences, functionality requirements, and any existing brand assets. The more details you can provide, the better I can understand your vision.

How do we communicate during the project?

I maintain regular communication through email, video calls, and project management tools. You'll receive weekly updates on progress, and I'm available to answer questions throughout the project timeline.

Do you offer website maintenance after launch?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages that include security updates, content updates, performance optimization, and technical support. These can be customized based on your specific needs.

What happens if I need changes after the project is complete?

Minor adjustments within the scope of the original project are included in my service. For larger changes or new features, we can discuss additional work at my standard hourly rate or as a new small project.
