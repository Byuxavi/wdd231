Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services

What is your design process?

My design process starts with understanding your needs and goals. Then I create wireframes and mockups for your approval before developing the final website. Throughout the process, I maintain clear communication and welcome your feedback.

How long does it take to complete a website?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects could take 1-2 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide website maintenance?

Yes, I offer website maintenance services to keep your site updated, secure, and performing well. Maintenance packages can include regular updates, security checks, backups, and content updates.

What information do you need to start a project?

To get started, I'll need information about your business/project, target audience, design preferences, content (text, images), functionality requirements, and any existing branding materials you have.
